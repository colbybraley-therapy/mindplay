How it works

Three steps to a richer session.

i.

Begin a session

Open the platform at the start of your appointment. Your client picks up where they left off — their character, their story, their progress.

ii.

Engage through story

Your client navigates character-driven scenarios built around their treatment focus. You observe, prompt, and guide — the platform creates space for disclosure.

iii.

Review the insights

After the session, a structured summary captures themes, choices, and language. Use it to inform your note and plan the next session.

Features

Designed for the work — not just the workflow.

Built around the choices, language, and patterns that actually move therapeutic work forward.

Built-in session structure

Each session follows a narrative arc that mirrors therapeutic models — creating natural moments for reflection, choice, and insight.

Therapist-facing client roster

Your dashboard shows every client's character, session activity, and XP at a glance — no digging required.

Session summaries

After every session, a structured summary captures the choices, language, and themes your client expressed — ready to inform your next note.

Eight therapeutic archetypes

Each character maps to a distinct clinical focus — from boundary-setting to identity formation — giving clients a resonant starting point.

Privacy-conscious by design

Client data stays in your practice. No third-party ad tracking, no data brokering — built for HIPAA-aligned clinical environments.

Questions

Frequently asked questions.

We are built for HIPAA-aligned environments. Client session data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We do not sell or broker client data. We recommend consulting your compliance officer for your specific practice context.
Cypress Mind is designed for clients ages 6–17. The characters and scenarios are calibrated developmentally — younger clients gravitate toward more concrete narrative choices, while adolescents engage with more nuanced identity and values-based scenarios.
No. Cypress Mind is designed to be used in-session on a shared device — a tablet or laptop in your office works well. Your client interacts with the platform during the session, and you review the summary on your therapist dashboard afterward.
Yes. Cypress Mind is a session engagement tool, not a replacement for your EHR. Session summaries are exportable and designed to complement your existing documentation workflow. EHR integration is on the product roadmap.
Plans are billed monthly, per seat. You can add or remove seats as your practice grows. During the pilot period, billing is handled directly — we'll reach out to set up payment once you're onboarded.
