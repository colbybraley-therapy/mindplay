How it works

A clinical workspace built around the therapeutic moment.

Three intentional steps replace the awkward warm-up, give clients agency, and surface the patterns that matter most for your work between sessions.

i.

Begin a session

Open Cypress Mind alongside your client. Start a new session, choose an activity, and let the platform handle the structure so you can focus on the relationship.

ii.

Engage through story

Clients build a character, choose paths, and respond to clinically-grounded scenarios. Every choice is data — language, preference, pattern, hesitation.

iii.

Review insights

Each session produces a structured summary you can scan, annotate, and carry forward. Track progress over time without losing the texture of the work.

Frequently asked

Cypress Mind is built for licensed therapists working with children and adolescents — typically ages 6 through 17. The platform is designed to be used during sessions alongside your client, with character-driven activities, structured session summaries, and a clinical workspace tailored to the therapeutic moment. It's not a replacement for clinical judgment; it's a tool that supports it.

Cypress Mind is built with HIPAA-aligned principles — encrypted in transit, minimum-data-collection, and strict authentication. Full HIPAA compliance, including a Business Associate Agreement, is included on the Group and Enterprise plans. Solo and Small Practice plans are appropriate for de-identified clinical engagement and pilot use; we recommend Group or Enterprise once you’re ready to flow real client PHI through the system.
